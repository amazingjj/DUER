Q:After resting they decided to go for a swim. The depth of the water is 15 times Dean's height. Dean is 4 feet taller than Ron. If Ron stands at 13 feet. How deep was the water?
A：Let's think step by step
1. Ron's height is 13 feet.
2. Dean is 4 feet taller than Ron.
3. Therefore, Dean's height is 13 + 4 = 17 feet.
4. The depth of the water is 15 times Dean's height.
5. To find the depth of the water, we multiply Dean's height by 15.
6. Therefore, the depth of the water is 15 * 17 = 255 feet.
The answer is 255

Q:Tiffany was collecting cans for recycling. On monday she had 4 bags of cans. The next day she found some more bags worth of cans. If she had a total of 6 bags altogether. How many bags did she find on the next day?
A：Let's think step by step
1. Tiffany had 4 bags of cans on Monday.
2. She found some more bags worth of cans the next day.
3. The total number of bags she had after finding more bags is 6.
4. To find out how many bags she found the next day, we need to subtract the number of bags she had on Monday from the total number of bags.
5. Therefore, the number of bags she found the next day is 6 - 4.
6. Performing the subtraction, we get 6 - 4 = 2.
The answer is 2

Q:The school is planning a field trip. The school has 87 classrooms. There are 58 students in the school with each classroom having the same number of students. If there are 2 seats on each school bus. How many buses are needed to take the trip?
A：Let's think step by step
1. There are 58 students in the school.
2. Each school bus has 2 seats.
3. To find out how many buses are needed, we need to divide the total number of students by the number of seats on each bus.
4. Therefore, the number of buses needed is 58 / 2.
5. Performing the division, we get 58 / 2 = 29.
The answer is 29

Q:After eating a hearty meal they went to see the Buckingham palace. There were 71 paintings in the Buckingham palace. There, Rachel learned that 557 visitors came to the Buckingham palace that day. If there were 188 visitors the previous day. How many visited the Buckingham palace within 57 days?
A：Let's think step by step
1. There were 557 visitors that day.
2. There were 188 visitors the previous day.
3. To find the number of more visitors that day than the previous day, we subtract the number of visitors the previous day from the number of visitors that day.
4. Therefore, the number of more visitors that day than the previous day is 557 - 188.
5. Performing the subtraction, we get 557 - 188 = 369.
6. To find the number of visitors within 57 days, we multiply the number of more visitors by 57.
7. Therefore, the number of visitors within 57 days is 369 * 57.
8. Performing the multiplication, we get 369 * 57 = 21033.
The answer is 21033

6. To find out how much Marco's dad's strawberries weigh now, we add the additional strawberries to the initial weight.

Q:Robin has some packages of gum. There are 7 pieces in each package. Robin has 6 extra pieces of gum. In all the number of pieces of gums robin has is 41. How many packages does Robin have?
A：Let's think step by step
1. Robin has 41 pieces of gum in total.
2. She has 6 extra pieces of gum.
3. To find the number of pieces of gum in packages, we subtract the extra pieces from the total number of pieces.
4. Therefore, the number of pieces of gum in packages is 41 - 6.
5. Performing the subtraction, we get 41 - 6 = 35.
6. Each package has 7 pieces of gum.
7. To find the number of packages, we divide the number of pieces of gum in packages by the number of pieces in each package.
8. Therefore, the number of packages Robin has is 35 / 7.
9. Performing the division, we get 35 / 7 = 5.
The answer is 5
